### Context

You are generating a complete LaTeX report based on **two input files**:

1. `./output/statistical_analysis.md` — structured quantitative output for all
   survey questions: frequency tables, Wilson CIs, Shannon entropy, chi-squared
   results, ordinal means, bootstrap CIs, positive percentages, and cross-analysis
   tables.

2. `./output/statistical_interpretations.md` — the per-question and per-cross-analysis

come from `statistical_analysis.md`. Every interpretive statement must be grounded in
`statistical_interpretations.md` or follow directly from the numbers. Where the
interpretation document provides the causal reasoning and the analysis document
provides the numbers, integrate both into coherent prose.

**2.3 Analytical Approach**
